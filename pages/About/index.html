Facts and History

FEU Tech History

In 1936, the Commonwealth was two years old and independence was promised a decade hence. The FEU, a private education pioneer, took stock of
the emergent the nation's needs. Among its findings was a very pressing need for engineers and technologists to help build the nation. It felt itself
challenged and it thus established the Institute of Technology.

An increasing demand for trained engineers and technologists remained. With the government’s accelerated economic recovery program, private
businesses establishing more and more factories, and agriculture shifting to mechanization, the demand for trained and skilled personnel was
greater than ever. The Institute of Technology helped by filling the country’s needs in this respect.

The renowned technical institutes and universities in the United States and Europe, including the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, University of Michigan, Cornell University,
and California Polytechnic, offered courses that led to a Bachelor of Science degree in a particular branch of engineering. These courses were somewhat similar to those offered by
the Institute of Technology. Nonetheless, they were tailored to the local environment and the demands of Philippine technical development.

Vision, Mission, and Quality Policy

Vision

In order to create the next generation of changemakers who will influence the future of the nation and the world,
FEU Institute of Technology strives to be the global standard for innovation in education, research, and nation-building.

Mission

FEU Institute of Technology is committed to provide high-quality, pertinent, creative, and industry-based education that results in capable,
morally upright professionals that are highly competitive, have a stronger sense of social responsibility, and are socially conscious.
These people will greatly improve society.

Quality Policy

Both FEU Tech and FEU Diliman are innovative institutions that provide each student a distinctive, high-quality educational experience. With its cutting-edge,
internationally relevant academic programs led by highly skilled, knowledgeable teachers, we give our students the technical and critical thinking abilities they need
to succeed as leaders in their areas. We shape the next generation of socially conscious and morally aware changemakers and country builders by adhering to the
basic values of Fortitude, Excellence, and Uprightness.

Facilities

Full library services, canteen and student services, state-of-the-art computers, engineering tools, and equipment,
air-conditioned classrooms and labs, round-the-clock security and monitoring, and WiFi connectivity throughout the facility
are all provided by FEU Tech.

Within the esteemed campus of Far Eastern University, which has played a significant role in Manila's history for more than 90 years,
is the FEU Technology Building, which is situated along Morayta. There are green parks and shaded trees on the Main Campus,
creating a natural biological setting. Award-winning artwork and architecture can also be found there, including five Art Deco structures created by Pablo
Antonio, a Filipino National Artist.

In the center of Manila's renowned University Belt, the FEU Institute of Technology is housed in a brand-new, 17-story structure. With its state-of-the-art elevators, electronic library facilities,
covered gym and basketball court, 25-meter indoor pool, eco-friendly Green Building Technology, e-Building high-tech security, standby power supply and generators,
and energy-efficient air conditioning, this brand-new building has all the amenities of a cutting-edge university.
